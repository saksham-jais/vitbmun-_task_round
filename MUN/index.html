Events

The "MUN Mate" event by the MUN Club at VIT Bhopal is an engaging platform for students to experience the essence of Model United Nations. Scheduled for October 16, 2024, the event includes several committees such as:

  • UNSC: Discussing global peace with a focus on the Middle East, including the Israel-Palestine conflict.

  • AIPPM: Addressing the statehood of Jammu, Kashmir, and Ladakh in India.

  • UNHRC: Protecting civilian rights in war zones.

  • UNCSW: Advocating for gender equality and reproductive rights.

  • UNGA: Tackling the global food security crisis.

  • IPC: Covering global events from a press perspective.

Participants will act as delegates, debate policies, and propose solutions to real-world issues. The event aims to enhance public speaking, diplomacy, and critical thinking skills​.
